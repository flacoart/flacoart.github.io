Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ's

Some commonly asked questions.

Do we have a registry?.

Yes! We're using Honeyfund to help fund our honeymoon! If you'd like to contribute, you can check it out here.

What is the dress code?

Cocktail and/or Beach Cocktail attire.

Where is parking?

Parking is available at the public lots for East Beach near the venue, but it is paid. We recommend using rideshare.

Can I bring a +1?

We'd love to celebrate with everyone, but we're keeping our guest list intimate. If your invitation includes a plus one, their name will be listed.

Are children invited?

We're keeping our celebration adult-only, with the exception of immediate famliy.

Will the wedding be indoors or outdoors?

The ceremony and cocktail hour will be held outside. The reception will take place indoors.

Will there be a bar?

Yes! Beer, wine, and two specialty cocktails will be on us. Tips are encouraged for the staff.

What time should I arrive?

The ceremony will begin punctually at 4:30pm. Please arrive no later than 4:20 pm.
Reception guests can arrive at 5:00 pm for cocktail hour at the Cabrillo Pavilion.
